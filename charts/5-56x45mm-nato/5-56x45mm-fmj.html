5.56x45mm FMJ

A .223 Remington (5.56x45mm) FMJ cartridge with a 3.6 gram lead core bullet with a bimetallic jacket in a steel case; intended for hunting, home defense, and target practice. Despite its rudimentary design and not having the full energy of an intermediate cartridge, the bullet is capable of pierce through basic ballistic body protections.
