7.62x39mm PP gzh

7N27 cartridge with improved penetration bullet. The "PP" bullet consists of a tompak-clad steel jacket with a truncated tip, a sharp core of hardened steel, a cap of thin metal foil coated with green varnish, and a lead jacket. The foil is used to prevent wear and tear on the gun and magazine when feeding cartridges.
