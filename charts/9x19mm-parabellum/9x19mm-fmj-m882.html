9x19mm FMJ M882

US Military 9mm ammunition is some of the most reliable ammunition available in the market, great for target shooting, plinking and practice. Loaded with "M882" 124-grain full metal jacket bullets rated at 1140 fps, this cartridge is considered +P (but slightly less than +P+) ammo. These increased pressure cartridges travel farther and penetrate better than standard 9mm rounds.
