4.6x30mm JSP SX

This is a 4.6x30 HK — Fibonacci 40gr. Jacketed Soft Point Ammo. This round is designed along the same lines as the 5.7x28 round. The jacketed soft point makes this a viable varmint and small game round. Usable in the H&K MP7. This ammo is brass cased, boxer primed, and non-corrosive.
